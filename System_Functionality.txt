create new account

/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////


    Screen 1:

    provide starting information

        choose account type
        select 1 or more interests
        provide zip code
        provide email address
        provide password

    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    Screen 2:

    provide information for member 1

        provide gender ( only for M/F couples - gender of member will
                         be known for the other 4 account types )

        provide year of birth
        provide sexual orientation
        provide race
        provide height
        provide body type
        provide occupation


    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    screen 2-B:

    provide information for member 2  ( this screen is only necessary
                                        for couple accounts )

        provide gender ( only for M/F couples - gender of member will
                         be known for the other 2 couple account types )

        provide year of birth
        provide sexual orientation
        provide race
        provide height
        provide body type
        provide occupation




    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    screen 3:

    confirm information is correct




    - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

    screen 4:

    confirm email address
